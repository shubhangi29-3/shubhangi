Hi, I am Divya Darshni Suresh!

A recent M.eng in Computer Science graduate from Cornell University.

Allow me to tell the story of my journey with an emphasis on my passion, commitment, and interest towards intersection of Artificial Intelligence and User Experience.

Throughout my academic career, I have been driven by a strong passion for technology and an unwavering commitment to my work. I am thrilled to embark on a career in the tech industry, where I can channel my enthusiasm and dedication into making a meaningful impact.

During my time at Cornell University, I discovered my affinity for User Experience (UX) and user research. Recognizing the importance of designing products and services that prioritize user needs and satisfaction, I delved into the world of UX design. Eager to expand my knowledge and skills in this field, I completed the Google certified UX Design Specialization course.

And for fueling my passion further, I continued exploring this fascinating realm by enrolling in courses such as Qualitative User Research and Design Methods, Human-Computer Interaction, and Design and Innovation at Cornell. These experiences not only deepened my understanding of UX principles but also nurtured my ability to approach projects with a user-centric mindset.

Later, I got an incredible opportunity to be part of SocialStools project as a Research Assistant. This project focused on studying the interaction between strangers in an playful social interaction setting fostering togetherness with interactive stools, employing user research methodologies. As on of the research assistant of the team, I actively contributed to the weekly tasks like transcriptions, data analysis like movement analysis, verbal analysis and non-verbal analysis to enable us to gain valuable insights into user behavior and preferences.

One of the highlights of my journey was the chance to combine my passions for AI and UX. For my M.eng project, I developed an application that aimed to enhance learning and education for children with autism. By leveraging the power of AI algorithms and UX design principles, I built a prototype that utilized machine learning techniques to screen for autism. This project exemplified my commitment to creating solutions that have a positive impact on the lives of others.

The story of my academic and professional pursuits is a testament to my unwavering enthusiasm and dedication. I am excited to continue exploring the ever-evolving world of technology, particularly in the realm of UX design and user research. With my diverse skill set, including a strong foundation in computer science and a passion for user-centric design, I am poised to make meaningful contributions in the field.

As I embark on this exciting chapter of my career, I am eager to leverage my skills and passion to drive innovation, improve user experiences, and create positive change.
