Compedible - Meal Prep Masterchef

Food plays a vital role in all our lives. As we have busy days ahead some of us tend to do meal prep which at times can come off as tiring or monotonous tasks.

For INFO - 5355 Human-Computer Interaction my teammates and I executed all the steps of user research like empathy, user research, ideate, design, and prototype.

We designed a meal prep application that was inspired by masterchef to give our users their masterchef moment. Our team later recieved the best user-centered design award (was aka the shut-up and take my money award :p) for the same during the project showcase.
